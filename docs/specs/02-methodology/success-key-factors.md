# Success Key Factors

> **Role in the project:** A personal and social compass for execution. These principles frame the mindset required to build Sding with discipline and resilience.
>
> **Leads to:** [Automated Solution Concept](../03-vision/automated-solution-concept.md)

---

## Personal

- **Delayed gratification — sacrificing immediate satisfaction for a greater future gain — is a hallmark of successful people.**
- **Developing an optimistic and resilient mindset is crucial.**
- **Focus on what has impact and eliminate distractions. Work hard, but efficiently.**
- **Read and write every day. Document what you do to avoid repeating mistakes and to progress faster.**
- **Understand and use the compound effect.** Small, consistent actions over time produce significant results.
- **Accept failure as part of the process.**
- **Be persistent.**
- **Stay focused on what you control: your actions ("output") rather than the results ("outcome").**
- **Stay disciplined by understanding the internal battle between your comfort zone and accepting difficulty.**
- **Do not neglect your wellbeing.**
- **Be willing to question yourself rather than looking for excuses when things get hard.**

## Social

- **Work with people who give you energy.**
- **Learn from others' mistakes.**
- **Stop comparing yourself to others and focus on your own path.**

## Core Message

- **Do the things most people won't do, to get the results most people will never get.**
- **Invest in your customers' success rather than focusing solely on the sale.**
